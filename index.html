Hello and Welcome to GardnerNotes.com, a WORK IN PROGRESS!

A Note About Jennifer Gardner, marketeer:

Jennifer refers to herself as a marketeer because not only does the extra e amuse her but it truly conveys her enthusiasm for marketing. Jennifer is excited to be a student in technical marketing track at the Startup Institute Chicago. Her career story began in Indiana at the Heartland Film Festival. As part of the small team running the Heartland Film Festival, Jennifer was responsible for writing film synopses and marketing materials, film programming and all things as needed. In Chicago, Jennifer has worked in various project management and administrative roles at a large law firm. For the HR department, Jennifer organized IT projects and the staff evaluation process including software customization, training and communications. She cannot wait to start the next chapter as a full-stack marketeer in a startup!

When she is not typing away at her computer, you will find her watching movies at Facets Cinematheque or the Siskel Film Center or cozied up on the couch watching The Mary Tyler Moore Show. She grew up in a DIY-oriented house (seamstress mother and mechanic father) and still enjoys taking things apart/putting them back together, sewing and crocheting.
